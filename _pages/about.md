## Research

Bone metastasis represents a highly dynamic and context-dependent ecosystem.  
I study:

- Remodeling of immune and stromal populations in metastatic bone lesions  
- Transcriptional reprogramming and alternative splicing during metastatic progression  
- Integration of GWAS / eQTL / sQTL / CNV with single-cell transcriptomics  
- Identification of regulatory drivers and potential therapeutic vulnerabilities  

My long-term goal is to bridge **genetic variation, single-cell state transitions, and therapeutic response prediction** in metastatic cancer.
